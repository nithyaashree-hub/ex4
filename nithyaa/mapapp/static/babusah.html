A.S.Babu Sah-Kanchipuram

A.S. Babu Sah is a well-known and established silk saree retailer in Kanchipuram, India.The business has been operating for a significant period, with its founding year noted as 1974.The emporium specializes in selling Kanchipuram silk sarees, including traditional and paithani designs with gold jari.
